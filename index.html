Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about QuanTix

What is QuanTix and who is it for?

+

QuanTix is a financial analytics and reporting platform built for accountants, business owners, investors, and financial analysts. It turns complex financial data into clear, actionable insights no accounting degree required.

Do I need accounting knowledge to use QuanTix?

+

Not at all. QuanTix is designed for everyone from seasoned accountants to business owners with no financial background. The platform translates numbers into plain language so you always know what's going on.

How does QuanTix keep my financial data secure?

+

Your data is encrypted end-to-end and stored on secure servers. QuanTix uses industry-standard security protocols to ensure your financial information is always protected and only accessible by you.

Can I generate reports automatically?

+

Yes. QuanTix auto-generates income statements, balance sheets, cash flow reports, and more with one click. Reports are clear, professionally formatted, and ready to share with stakeholders instantly.

Is there a free trial available?

+

Yes you can get started with QuanTix for free. No credit card required. Explore the dashboard, analytics, and reporting tools before choosing a plan that fits your business.

Can multiple team members access the platform?

+

Absolutely. QuanTix supports team collaboration you can invite accountants, analysts, and stakeholders to view or manage financial data based on their role and permission level.
